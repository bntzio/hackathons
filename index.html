Introducing a new way to outsource innovation

We are a company focused on creating products that solve real world problems of companies, big or small.
We achieve this through our innovation first approach.

How we do it


Step 1: Pain Points

We look for the biggest needs the company is currently facing. We do this through an intensive process of actively interviewing, analyzing, and consulting different areas of the company until we find practices that can be improved through technology.

Step 2: Brief

With the information gathered, we make a brief. This brief works as a especialized guide for the participants who take place at our hackathon. They will be aware of all the specifics of the needs the company is facing currently.

Step 3: Hackathon

This is where the solution is born. When you put together a group of designers, hackers, thinkers, and innovators in a room for a 24 hour period, magic happens.

Step 4: Implementation

After we've come up with several prototypes, we analyze which of them is the most viable to solve the previously posted problems. This is done through an itereative process with our client.
