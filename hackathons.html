I/O Hack

I/O Hack was a Hackathon with three main challenges: Agriculture, Logistics, and Retail. All of which had a focus on the Internet of Things. 60 of the best hackers in our country came to participate and try to figure out how to solve these challenges. We had drones, we had RC Cars, we had sensors, we had AI, we had everything.
